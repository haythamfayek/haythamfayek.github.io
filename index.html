Hi there!

I am a VCPS PhD student at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, RMIT University. My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Intelligent Systems; with an emphasis on Deep Learning and Neural Networks and their applications in Speech Processing and Affective Computing. I have previously worked with Fuzzy Logic, Bio-Inspired Optimization, Computer Vision, Control Theory, Robotic Control and Renewable Energy.

Formerly, I received an MSc and a BEng in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the Petronas University of Technology and worked as an Electrical / Electronics Engineer for three years. I am a member of the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE) and the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
